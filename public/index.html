WHO WE ARE

Pioneering Energy Excellence Since Day One

East Coast Sustainable was founded by experienced professionals with an impressive pedigree in energy efficiency, renewable energy, environment conservation & climate change. East Coast Sustainable is an Empanelled Accredited Energy Auditing Firm (EmAEA) by Bureau of Energy Efficiency, India for conducting Monitoring & Verification Audits and Check Verification Audits under the PAT scheme. Our team holds eminent certifications including Certified Energy Auditor, Accredited Energy Auditor, Lead Auditor (ISO 9001 & ISO 50001) & Chartered Engineer.

  • Empanelled Accredited Energy Auditing Firm (EmAEA) by BEE, India
  • 700+ Projects across industries, government & institutions
  • Certified Energy Auditors | Lead Auditors ISO 9001 & ISO 50001
  • Multisector reach: industry, government, NGOs, financial institutions
